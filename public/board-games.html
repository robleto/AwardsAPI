Example Requests

Get award by ID:

/api?i=10865&apikey=yourkey

Search for "Spiel des Jahres":

/api?s=Spiel%20des%20Jahres&apikey=yourkey

Get all awards for a game:

/api?bgg_id=266192&apikey=yourkey

Awards from 2020:

/api?s=winner&year=2020&apikey=yourkey
