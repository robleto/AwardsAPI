Available Parameters

imdb_id IMDb ID (e.g. tt0133093)
title Film title to search for
year Year of film release
ceremony Award ceremony name
category Award category
person Person name (actor, director, etc.)
badges_only Get badge summary (true/false)

Example Requests

Get awards by IMDb ID:

/film-awards?imdb_id=tt0133093&apikey=yourkey

Search by title:

/film-awards?title=Oppenheimer&year=2023&apikey=yourkey

Get award badges only:

/film-awards?imdb_id=tt0133093&badges_only=true&apikey=yourkey

Filter by ceremony:

/film-awards?ceremony=Academy%20Awards&year=2024&apikey=yourkey
